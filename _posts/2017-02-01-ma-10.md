## Graphs

Definition

A graph G = (V,E) consists of V, vertex set, any set. 

$$E \subseteq \binom{V}{2}$$, edge set. 

### Standard examples

1) Complete graph $$K_{n}$$

has V = $$\{1,2,...,n\}$$

$$E = \binom{V}{2}$$
