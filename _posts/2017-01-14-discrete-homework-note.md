* 18
a) Prove $$A∪B⊆A∪B∪C$$ for any sets A,B,C

Solution: Let x be arbitrary.

$$x∈A∪B → (x∈A∪B)∨(x∈C)$$
	$$→ x∈(A∪B)∪C$$ [Definition of $$\BigCup$$]

Thus, since $$x ∈ A ∪ B → x ∈ (A ∪ B) ∪ C$$, it follows that $$A ∪ B ⊆ A ∪ B ∪ C$$, **by definition of
subset**.
